Understanding Data Priorities

Priority 1 Data (Green): Trip to change to back-up tool. Can only be dropped with the agreement of the Wells Superintendent, Rig-Line SPA, Depletion Management TL, New Well Delivery TL and Drilling Engineering TL. Completions Engineering TL to be consulted.

Priority 2 Data (Yellow): Proceed without this data if the tool fails but change out if the opportunity arises. Nevertheless, Wells Superintendent, Rig-Line SPA, Depletion Management TL, New Well Delivery TL and Drilling Engineering TL may elect to acquire the data, depending on the criticality of the log. Completions Engineering TL to be consulted.

Note (*): Conditional activation. Subject to be activated during drilling if needed. For ALD Image specifically: can be activated during drilling if the need is identified.
